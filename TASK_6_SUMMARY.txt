ROOT CAUSE:
The issue was caused by shift-reduce conflicts from the ambiguous grammar rule:
  postfix_expr ::= IDENTIFIER LBRACE struct_init_list RBRACE

This rule made parser behavior unpredictable in complex contexts. When nested
for loops were combined with error returns, defer blocks, and suspend statements,
the cumulative effect of shift-reduce decisions could cause parse failures.

THE FIX (from task-summary-4):
Removed the ambiguous rule and rely on: type LBRACE (where type ::= IDENTIFIER)
Result: Reduced conflicts from 122 to 119, made parser more deterministic
